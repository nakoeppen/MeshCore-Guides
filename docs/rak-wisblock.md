## General Notes
This board comes in two main variants, particularly the RAK19003 (WisBlock Mini) or the RAK19007 (WisBlock). **Please be aware that the battery port has the polarity switched compared to most traditional JST PHR-2 connectors.** Be sure to check [Official RAK Documentation](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/wisblock/rak19003/quickstart/#battery-connection) for more details.
